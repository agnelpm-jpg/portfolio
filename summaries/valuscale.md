# ValuScale: Algorithmic Pricing Framework

## Abstract

ValuScale is a service-pricing framework that replaces gut-feeling invoices with structured business evaluation. It estimates price from value, affordability, complexity, and implementation risk.

## Core Thesis

The wrong pricing question is: "What should I charge?"

The better question is:

\[
\text{Price} = f(\text{Value Created},\text{Affordability},\text{Complexity},\text{Risk})
\]

## Model Sketch

A practical scoring model can be written as:

\[
P = B \cdot V_w \cdot C_w \cdot R_w \cdot A_w
\]

where:

- \(B\) is the base service cost.
- \(V_w\) weights expected business value.
- \(C_w\) weights operational complexity.
- \(R_w\) weights delivery and uncertainty risk.
- \(A_w\) adjusts for client affordability and sustainability.

## Intended Use

ValuScale is meant for service businesses, agencies, consultants, and system builders who need explainable pricing. It turns pricing from negotiation theater into a transparent decision model.
